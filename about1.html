Why Invest in Sikkim?

Sikkim, India's first organic state and one of the most peaceful regions, offers unparalleled opportunities for investors. With its strategic location bordering three countries, special economic incentives, and commitment to sustainable development, Sikkim is the ideal destination for businesses looking to grow in harmony with nature.

  • Tax holidays and exemptions for new industries
  • 100% FDI allowed in most sectors
  • Single window clearance system
  • Abundant hydropower potential
  • Skilled and educated workforce
  • Political stability and peaceful environment
  • Growing infrastructure development
  • Gateway to ASEAN markets
